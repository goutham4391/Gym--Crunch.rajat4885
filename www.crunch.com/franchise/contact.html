So at this point, you're probably saying to yourself "I'm ready! Let's do this!" Learn how you get started and what the process looks like.

1. Complete Our Request for Information

For information on purchasing a Crunch Fitness Franchise, complete our form on this page, or contact us by phone at 800.669.7162. A member of our Franchise Development Team will contact you to set up a time to discuss the Crunch opportunity with you.

2. Submit a Franchise Application

After your conversation with our development team, you'll receive instructions on how to complete our franchise application to determine your financial qualifications.

3. Participate in our Crunch Franchise Webinar

Once your franchise application has been approved, you'll have the opportunity to dive deeper into the entire process of opening and operating your own franchise via an online webinar.

4. Conduct Your Due Dilligence

Upon successful completion of our webinar, you can download and review our Franchise Disclosure Document (FDD), which provides a detailed investment breakdown, financial performance and fee structure. We will also have you speak with current franchisees and answer any questions you may have so you can complete your due diligence.

5. Meet Our Executive Team

The final step before making a decision is to meet with our executive team and tour an existing franchise location. After this meeting, you can make an informed decision on taking the next step. If everything looks like a fit and you decide to become a part of the Crunch Family, we'll draw up the Franchise Agreement.

6. Execute Your Franchise Agreement

Review and sign the agreement, and you'll be on your way. You will have joined the best and most dynamic brand in the industry - Crunch Fitness!
